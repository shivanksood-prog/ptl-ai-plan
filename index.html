The Problem
Humans doing machine work
L1 agents spend most of their time looking up data and reading it to partners. When they can't solve it, the partner repeats everything to L2.
Low Compliance
Process depends on human discipline. 17% of tickets dumped in "Some Other Issue" — a catch-all for when agents don't know where to categorize.
Slow Resolution
Partner explains problem to L1. L1 can't solve. Partner waits. L2 calls back. Partner explains again. Resolution target: 24 hours.
Data Lookups by Humans
Most calls are "check recharge status", "give PPPoE", "what's my device status" — answers that exist in systems already.
The Plan
Two months. Two milestones.
Build the brain first. Then decide — human reads the answer, or AI speaks it.

Month 2 — The Voice
The brain gets a mouth
Same answer engine. The choice is who delivers the answer — AI or human. You pick per category based on compliance vs cost.

You don't pick one.
AI voice for high-volume, simple categories. Human + brain for everything else. Move categories from human → AI one at a time as confidence builds.
Risk Mitigation
Why this won't be a disaster
01
Month 1 is invisible
Partners see no change. Same human picks up, same process. It's just faster and more accurate internally.
02
Month 2 is opt-in
Move one category at a time to AI voice. If it fails, that category goes back to human. No big bang switch.
03
Every failure teaches
"No Answer" cases become new categories. Low-PSAT calls get reviewed. The brain grows smarter with every call.
